Hi, I’m someone who finds comfort in daily routines and joy in the small things. Every day starts the same for me: a warm shower, breakfast with yogurt and fruit, and a quiet moment with a cup of coffee. I wake up around 8:30 AM — not super early, but early enough to ease into the day. I spend a lot of time behind a screen — more than 11 hours most days. Some of that is for work or learning, but a big part of it is gaming, exploring ideas, and just being curious about the world. Music is always present. It helps me focus, relax, or just enjoy the moment — more than 10 hours a week, at least. I try to take care of myself: I drink about 1.5 liters of water a day, and going for walks is something I really enjoy. Being outside, even briefly, helps me recharge. And animals? I love them. Especially dogs, cats, and chickens — they make life a little softer. I guess if I had to sum myself up: I’m someone who values balance, creativity, and staying true to who I am. This little dataset says a lot about me — not just what I do, but how I live.
